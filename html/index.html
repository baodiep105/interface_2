Fatfish Restaurant

Fatfish was created by gastronomes, for gastronomes! Our concept is simple: fresh, stunning quality ingredients, prepared with passion . We offer contemporary cuisine in a stylish yet relaxed riverfront venue, and are proud to be ranked as Lonely Planet’s top pick for dining in Da Nang. With our own in house bakery, outstanding craft beers, and a carefully selected wine and cocktail list, Fatfish is the place to indulge your taste buds!
